SMS Messaging Consent

Last updated: April 3, 2026

What you're signing up for

By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive SMS messages from Baseline Health ("Baseline") related to your personalized health coaching program. Messages are sent by Milo, your AI health coach.

Types of messages

You may receive the following types of messages:

  • Daily health check-ins and habit reminders
  • Morning briefs with sleep and wellness summaries
  • Evening check-ins on protocol adherence
  • Weekly health reviews with metric trends
  • Responses to messages you send to your coach

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your coaching program. Typically 1 to 3 messages per day. You can adjust your preferences at any time by messaging your coach.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Baseline does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may.

By opting in, you consent to the following:

I agree to receive recurring automated SMS messages from Baseline Health at the phone number I provide. I understand that consent is not a condition of purchase. I can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, I can reply HELP or contact us at support@mybaseline.health.

You're signed up. You'll receive a confirmation text from Milo shortly. Reply STOP at any time to opt out.

How to opt out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from Baseline. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. After opting out, you will not receive any further messages unless you re-subscribe.

How to get help

Reply HELP to any message from Baseline for assistance, or contact us at support@mybaseline.health.

Supported carriers

Baseline SMS works with all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your phone number will only be used to send you health coaching messages. We will never sell, share, or use your phone number for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
