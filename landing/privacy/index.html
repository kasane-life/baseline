Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

What Baseline is

Baseline is a personal health analytics tool that scores your health data against US population benchmarks (NHANES). It helps you understand where you stand and what to focus on.

Your data stays on your device

All health data you enter — lab results, vitals, wearable metrics, medications, demographics — is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. Your health data is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

  • No health data is sent to any server, cloud, or third party
  • No account creation required
  • No cookies are used for tracking
  • Clearing your browser data removes all stored health information

Voice transcription

If you use the voice input feature, your speech is processed by your browser's built-in speech recognition (Web Speech API). The transcript may be sent to our AI extraction service to parse health values from natural language. Only the transcript text is sent — no audio is transmitted. Transcripts are not stored after processing.

Wearable data

If you connect a wearable device (Garmin, Apple Health, Oura, or others), data is imported with your explicit consent. Imported data is processed client-side and stored locally in your browser, the same as manually entered data. We do not store, sell, or share your wearable data.

  • Garmin: If you connect via Garmin Connect, we request read-only access to your daily health summaries (steps, sleep, heart rate, HRV, VO2 max). We do not modify your Garmin data.
  • Apple Health: Data is imported via file export or iOS bridge. No data is sent to our servers.
  • Oura: If you connect via Oura, we request read-only access to your sleep, activity, and readiness data.

Google Calendar

If you connect your Google Calendar, we request access to your calendar events (the calendar.events scope). This allows your coach to:

  • Read your schedule to provide time-aware coaching (meal timing, workout scheduling, sleep protection)
  • Create events on your behalf (training sessions, health appointments, reminders)
  • Update or delete events you ask your coach to change

Calendar data is accessed in real time when your coach needs it. We do not bulk-download, cache, or permanently store your calendar events. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest and stored on your dedicated server instance. You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time by revoking access in your Google Account settings.

We do not sell, share, or use your calendar data for advertising. Calendar access is used solely to provide personalized health coaching.

Baseline's use of Google Calendar data adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

What we do collect

We may collect anonymous, non-health usage data to improve the product:

  • Page views and feature usage (e.g., which sections are used most)
  • Error logs for debugging (no health data included)
  • Discovery form responses (optional product feedback you choose to submit)

Third-party services

  • Formspree: Handles optional email signup and feedback form submissions
  • Cloudflare Workers: Powers voice transcript parsing and AI extraction
  • Anthropic (Claude): Used for AI-assisted data extraction from voice transcripts. Only transcript text is sent, never raw health data
  • Google Calendar API: Reads and writes calendar events for health coaching, with your explicit OAuth consent. See the Google Calendar section above for details.

Data deletion

Since your data is stored locally, you control it completely. Use the "Start fresh" option in the app to clear all stored data, or clear your browser's site data for this domain.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
