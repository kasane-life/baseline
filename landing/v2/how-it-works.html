HOW IT
WORKS

Four steps. No app to download. Your coach meets you where you are.

FOUR
STEPS
01

You sign up with your name and number. That's it. Your coach reaches out on whatever channel you prefer and gets to know you. WhatsApp, iMessage, Telegram, SMS. No app download, no account creation.

02

Your coach builds your health picture. Over time, not all at once. Maybe it starts with sleep. Maybe you have labs you want to make sense of. The system maps what you have, identifies what's missing, and prioritizes what to measure next.

03

You get coached on what matters. The system finds the highest-leverage thing for you right now and helps you lock it in before moving to the next. One habit at a time. No overwhelm.

04

You see real outcomes. Not charts. Changes. Better sleep. More energy. A coach that notices progress and adjusts with you. Daily check-ins adapt based on your data and goals.

SIMPLE
EQUIPMENT

You don't need expensive lab panels to get started. A few inexpensive tools cover most of the top metrics. Several of the highest-value measurements are completely free.

Scale
~$25

Daily weigh-ins create your rolling average. The trend is the signal, not any single day.

daily · morning, post-bathroom
Blood Pressure Monitor
~$40

Home readings are more accurate than clinical ones. Tracks systolic, diastolic, and resting heart rate.

2-3x per week · morning, seated 5 min
Tape Measure
~$3

Waist circumference is one of the strongest predictors of metabolic risk. Simple and free after purchase.

weekly · at navel, morning
Wearable
optional

Garmin, Apple Watch, or Oura. Unlocks sleep quality, HRV, resting heart rate, and step count. Not required to get started.

continuous · auto-synced
(for developers)
USE WITH
CLAUDE

The Baseline scoring engine runs as an MCP server. Add it to Claude Desktop and get your health score, log data, and track trends directly in conversation.

claude_desktop_config.json
{
  "mcpServers": {
    "health-engine": {
      "command": "uvx",
      "args": [
        "--from", "kiso",
        "health-engine-mcp"
      ],
      "env": {
        "HEALTH_ENGINE_USER": "your-name"
      }
    }
  }
}

Install with a single command:

uvx --from kiso health-engine-mcp

Requires Python 3.11+. Scoring engine is open source.
