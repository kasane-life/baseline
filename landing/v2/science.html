(what we catch that others miss)
INDIVIDUAL METRICS LIE.
PATTERNS DON'T.

Every metric in range doesn't mean healthy. The system checks five compound patterns that cross metrics. These are the things a dashboard can't see and a single lab review misses.

Metabolic Syndrome

Waist + triglycerides + HDL + blood pressure + glucose. Three of five criteria triggers a flag. Each metric can be "borderline normal." Together, they mean something.

Insulin Resistance

HOMA-IR calculation from fasting glucose + insulin. TG/HDL atherogenic ratio. Ferritin as a metabolic marker. Shows up 10-15 years before a diabetes diagnosis.

Recovery-Deficit Cascade

Sleep under target + high bedtime variance + elevated RHR + suppressed HRV. Four signals that compound. Fix sleep regularity and the rest often resolves.

Atherogenic Dyslipidemia

High ApoB + low HDL + high triglycerides. The lipid pattern most predictive of cardiovascular events. Standard cholesterol panels miss it.

Recovery Stress

HRV trending down + RHR trending up + sleep duration dropping. Your body is telling you something before you feel it. The numbers move before symptoms show.
