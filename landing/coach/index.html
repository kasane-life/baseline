There's a healthier version of you on the other side of this

We built something new. A health coach that knows your numbers, connects them, and drives you to outcomes. One thing at a time, at your pace, starting from wherever you are right now.

What this is

A coach that meets you where you are and guides you forward. It reaches out on WhatsApp, iMessage, text, whatever you use. It learns your health picture, figures out what matters most right now, and helps you act on it.

It knows your sleep last night, your weight trend this month, and your lab results from last quarter. It connects all of it and tells you the one thing to focus on today.

You don't need to bring anything to get started. No wearable required. No lab results needed upfront. Just your name and a phone number. We'll guide you through what your health context looks like and build from there.

How it works

  1. You sign up with your name and number. That's it. Your coach reaches out on whatever channel you prefer and gets to know you. Where you're at, what you care about, what you're working on.
  2. Your coach helps you build your health picture. Over time, not all at once. Maybe it starts with sleep and a morning weigh-in. Maybe you have labs you want to make sense of. We meet you where you are.
  3. You get coached on what matters. Foundations first. Sleep, movement, nutrition, recovery. The system finds the highest-leverage thing for you right now and helps you lock it in before moving to the next.
  4. You see real outcomes. Not charts. Changes. Better sleep. More energy. Weight moving in the right direction. A coach that notices progress and adjusts with you.

Your data

This is a beta. We're a small team building in the open. Your health data is stored locally and never sold, shared, or used for advertising. The scoring engine is open source. We do our best to keep everything secure, and we'll always be straight with you about how your information is handled.
